About AgentNet

Learn more about AgentNet

AgentNet is an educational website created in 2026 that focuses on explaining how artificial intelligence is used in everyday life. The website was developed as part of an academic project.

Artificial intelligence is used in many technologies people interact with daily, such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, and smart devices. AgentNet aims to explain these technologies in a simple and easy-to-understand way.

Who Is This Website For?

This website is designed for students, beginners in technology and members of the general public who want to learn more about artificial intelligence without needing technical knowledge.

Purpose of the Website

The purpose of AgentNet is to provide clear information about how AI impacts everyday life and help users understand where and how it is being used.
